About me

I've led teams that build online help, developer documentation, UI text, in-product tutorials, websites, and more for over a decade.

When I began my first role as a technical writer, I was fascinated by an early feedback loop: we had a team of smart, dedicated people writing help content, but our content wasn't helpful and our users couldn't find what they were looking for. What were we doing wrong? It turns out this problem wasn't unique to us. The findability and usability of information remains one of the most significant and underinvested UX problems of the past decade.

I came to see content as a design discipline, and recognized that, like the products we build, content can be tested, iterated upon, and improved using agile, data-driven methodologies. This philosophy has helped me make content a differentiator that customers have praised.

I'm based in Vancouver. When I'm not working, I love to travel, play video games, study history and technology, and cook.
