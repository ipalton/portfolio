In-product guides

We improved adoption, engagement, and the perception of value in our SaaS platform with context-aware, just-in-time learning, at scale.

The Problem

We had a comprehensive SaaS platform with a large number of legacy and new capabilities. It served a number of different types of users. Some capabilities were role-specific, and others were shared between the roles.

  • We constantly launched new features, but the people who should have been using those features were not.
  • When talking to product managers, our customers were often surprised to learn the platform had a solution to their challenge, but they didn't know about it.
  • After activating their account, people entered the platform to see at a list of applications. There was no clarity on what they should do first and no guidance directing them to high-value activities that improved engagement and prevented churn.
